About

last updated 28 April 2026

I am a theoretical physicist interested in quantum field theory in curved spacetimes and gravitation, currently pursuing a PhD at U. of Valencia and U. of Genoa.

The purpose of this page is to serve as a platform for both the academic and non-academic work. While academic work is easy to find, I have sporadically done cool stuff that I cannot share in any other place, like posters or inknertia, a Typst library to draw physics diagrams!

I am also quite fond of bringing science closer to the general public, so you might find here a collection of outreach-y physics topics which I never bother sharing with people as a .pdf, but might be cute to send as .html. I have also dabbled with cybersecurity and computer science, so watch out for that.

As for my background, I graduated from U. of Murcia, Spain—where I met some amazing people—and then got an MSc in Advanced Physics at U. of Valencia. Beyond science, I love listening to music, traveling and spending time with my friends :).
